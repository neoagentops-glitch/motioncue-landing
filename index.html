Say hello

Meet Stanley.

The calm little figure in every stretch has a name.

Stanley shows you each move from the inside: he sits where you sit, holds what you hold, and turns so you can follow along without a second guess. Mirror him and good form just happens.

He is pared down to a few quiet shapes on purpose. The minimalism is the design, not the ceiling: a companion you can copy without thinking, never a mascot demanding your attention.

Better with a little company

Keep the habit with people you like.

A Circle is a small, private group you invite. See who has moved today, send a cheer, feel one land on your wrist. No feeds, no followers, no scores to chase.

  • Private, by invite only. Only the people you invite are in a Circle, and you can keep a few at once: family in one, work in another.
  • A name and a streak, nothing more. Your Circle sees the display name you choose, your streak, and whether you moved today. Never which stretches you did, your schedule, or your hydration.
  • Cheers, not leaderboards. Tap to send "Love it," "Proud of you," or "Nice work." It blooms on their card and buzzes gently on their wrist.
  • Bring a friend in free. Someone you invite skips the upfront paywall and can start moving right away.
