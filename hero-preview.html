Indented left rule: pick the color

The left rule, inset so it reads as a deliberate pull-quote. 1-6 are the colors (elegant ones keep the text dark and color the rule plus one verb; anchor ones color the whole line). 7-9 are variations on #1: 7 puts gold on "Sitting still" too; 8 and 9 swap the gold for a different eye-drawing color (warm coral, cool blue). All three break the line after "all day" so the intervention lands on its own. 10 and 11 are your picks (1 and 7) with "MotionCue" in the brand green, so the line carries the gold accent and the signature green together. 12 is 7 with about a half-line of extra breath between the two lines. The sub-line now reads "phone or wrist" throughout, since the app is iPhone and Apple Watch. Unlisted; nothing here is on the live site.
